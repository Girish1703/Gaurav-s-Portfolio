ASME Standard B16.5 The ASME valve standard outlines technical specifications and requirements governing valve design, materials, manufacturing, testing, and performance in industrial applications. It sets criteria for valve dimensions, pressure-temperature ratings, end connections, and sealing mechanisms to ensure reliability across diverse operating conditions.

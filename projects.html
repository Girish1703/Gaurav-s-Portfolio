"Pendulum Operated Water Pump"

Drive Link: Drive Link

Description: * In my diploma final year project, I developed a novel solution for water pumping using a pendulum-operated mechanism inspired by Newton's third law. The design ingeniously harnesses the swinging motion of a pendulum through a connecting rod to drive the reciprocating action of the water pump. * This innovative approach eliminates the need for electricity, making it a cost-effective and energy-efficient solution for water pumping. The system not only showcases a sustainable alternative but also addresses the challenges of remote or off-grid locations where conventional power sources may be impractical.
